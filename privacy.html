Privacy Policy — YS Tax Calculator

Version 1.0 · Last updated: 17 July 2026

Yourshield Solutions LTD ("we", "us"), a company registered in the Republic of Cyprus, operates the YS Tax Calculator mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what data the App handles.

The short version

All tax calculations happen locally on your device. The App does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. It contains no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Data you enter

Income, family status, and other figures you enter are used solely to perform the calculation on your device. They never leave your device and are not accessible to us. Your preferences (language, theme, selected tax year) are stored locally on your device only.

2. Data we collect

None. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and does not transmit your data over the network.

3. Purchases

If you buy a subscription or a one-time purchase, payment is processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play — we never receive your payment details. To recognize your purchase (activate paid features and restore them on a new device), the App uses RevenueCat, a purchase-management service. It processes an anonymous, randomly generated identifier and your purchase history (product, date, status). This identifier is not linked to your identity, is not used for tracking or advertising, and serves only to provide the features you bought. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy. You may request deletion of this purchase data by contacting us (section 8).

4. Third parties

The only third parties involved are Apple / Google (payments and app distribution) and RevenueCat (purchase recognition). No other third party receives any data from the App.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

Because the App does not collect personal data, most GDPR obligations are satisfied by design. For anything related to purchase data held via RevenueCat (access, deletion), contact us and we will process your request.

6. Children

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy (for example, if a future version adds features). The current version is always available at this address and inside the App; the "Last updated" date shows the latest revision.

8. Contact

Yourshield Solutions LTD, Republic of Cyprus. Email: seo@yourshield.com.cy

In case of discrepancy between language versions, the English version prevails.
