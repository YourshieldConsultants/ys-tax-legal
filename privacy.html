Privacy Policy — YS Tax Calculator

Version 1.1 · Last updated: 20 July 2026

Yourshield Solutions LTD ("we", "us"), a company registered in the Republic of Cyprus, operates the YS Tax Calculator mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what data the App handles.

The short version

All tax calculations happen locally on your device. The App does not transmit your tax data or calculation results anywhere. It contains no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking. Automatic network requests are made only when you open the News tab.

1. Data you enter

Income, family status, and other figures you enter are used solely to perform the calculation on your device. They never leave your device and are not accessible to us. Your preferences (language, theme, selected tax year) are stored locally on your device only.

2. Data we collect

We do not receive your tax data or calculation results. We run no servers that receive your data, and the App has no user accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. What you enter and the results stay on your device and leave it only through an action you explicitly take (see section 4). To show the News tab, the App loads a news feed and images from our public server (GitHub Pages); these are ordinary web requests that carry no tax data, but — as with visiting any website — the hosting provider sees your device's IP address.

3. Purchases

The App is free. It has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features, and it uses no purchase-management service. No payment or purchase data is processed.

4. External links and sharing

The App does not transmit anything without an explicit action by you. Some of your actions open, or hand data to, something outside the App:

  • News sources — tapping a news item's source link opens that website in your browser.
  • Contact on WhatsApp — the contact button opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message to Yourshield's business number. The pre-filled text is handed to WhatsApp as soon as the link opens, but the business recipient sees it only if you actually send it.
  • Share / export — the system share action lets you send your calculation result, as text or as a PNG/PDF file, to an app or contact you choose; for PNG or PDF a temporary file is created for the share. The result goes where you send it and does not come to us.

In each case you start the action and choose the destination.

5. Third parties

Apple / Google distribute the App through their stores, and GitHub hosts our public news feed (so it sees standard request metadata, such as your IP address, when the News tab loads). Beyond these, the only parties that can receive anything are the ones you choose through the actions in section 4 — the source website you open, WhatsApp, or the recipient you pick when sharing. No third party receives your tax data or calculation results unless you send it yourself.

6. Data retention

The tax figures you enter are not stored permanently — they exist only in the App's memory while you use it and are gone once the App closes. Stored separately on your device are: your preferences (language, theme, tax year), kept until you change them or uninstall the App; the News cache, kept so the News tab works offline, overwritten by newer content and removed when you uninstall the App; and temporary export files (PNG or PDF), created only when you share and cleared by the system. None of this is held by us.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Because we hold none of your personal data and your tax data is processed only on your device, there is no personal data on our side to access, correct, or delete. If you have any privacy question, contact us (section 10).

8. Children

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy (for example, if a future version adds features). The current version is always available at this address and inside the App; the "Last updated" date shows the latest revision.
